How it works

A diary you talk into.

One

Press the button and say how your day went, for about twenty seconds.

Your phone writes it down as a few lines in fountain-pen blue.

Three

Kept

Each day is filed in a private notebook. Look back by month or by year.

Private by default

The promise

Your words stay on your phone.

Your voice becomes text on the device itself. Your entries are stored on your iPhone, not on our computers. We do not have accounts, we do not read your diary, and we do not sell anything about you. Read the full privacy policy.
